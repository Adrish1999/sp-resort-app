About Us

Swapno Puran Resort is located in Jotgiri, Howrah, only 13 kilometers away from the holy river Ganga. It is a luxurious property that will make you feel like royalty. There is plenty of artwork, home theater, balcony, parking on property, a bar, and fireplaces. Swimming pool, picnic area, 24 hour security, garden, and gym are the amenities included. It also has appliances such as refrigerator/freezer, microwave, oven, electricity, ceiling fans, water purification system , and a water heater. Swapno Puran Resort is a perfect balance between comfort, luxury, and convenience, so come and enjoy all what Swapno Puran Resort has to offer!

BELUR MATH

This monastery, which is located in Howrah, is dedicated to Sri Ramakrishna. It was previously known as the Baranagar Math. Later, in 1899, it was changed to Belur by Vivekananda. It is located near the Hooghly River. And, it is only around 16 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.

DAKSHINESHWAR KALI TEMPLE

Dakshineswar Kali Temple, majestic 19th-century temple in the village of Dakshineswar, outside Kolkata, India, on the east bank of the Hugli River, an arm of the Ganges. The presiding deity is Bhavatarini, an aspect of the goddess Kali. The temple is around 14.8 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.

HOWRAH BRIDGE

Also known as the Rabindra Setu, Howrah Bridge is one of the major tourist attractions in Howrah and it is one of the busiest bridges, which is constructed on the Hooghly River, connecting Kolkata city with Howrah. The best time to visit this bridge is during nighttime because at night Howrah Bridge is lit with bright lights to showcase its beauty. Only 12.3 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.

ADYAPEETH TEMPLE

Adyapeath is another Hindu Temple which was built in 1967. The main altar of the temple is having three sub-altars. The bottom is having the statue of Sri Ramkrishna, the middle is having Maa Adya and the top altar displays Radha and Krishna.Only 15.3 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.

SHIBPUR BOTANICAL GARDEN

Acharya Jagadish Chandra Bose Indian Botanic Garden, which contains over 12,000 plants, is one of the most popular tourist sites in the region. It also houses the largest and oldest banyan tree in the world. The gardens are a paradise for botanists and can be enjoyed for a walk or picnic in the lush greenery.Only 15.9 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.

SWAPNO PURAN RESORT

VIDYASAGAR SETU

This bridge is located on the Hooghly River and connects Kolkata and Howrah. It is also the longest in Asia and the longest cable bridge in India. The bridge was named after Pandit Ishwar Chandra Vidyasagar, a former Bengali educationist.Only 14 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.

GANGA AARTI AT GHATS

The aarti, or worshipping River Ganga, takes place every day, at dusk. The ceremony is performed by a group of priests on the ghats. Amid blowing of conch shells, the ringing of several bells, the clanging of brass cymbals and the chanting chorus of mantras, the priests venerate Ganga. The Ganga is only 13 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.

SWAPNO PURAN RESORT

HOWRAH RAILWAY MUSEUM

A miniature Howrah railway station can be found inside the museum. The Rail Museum Howrah has preserved an antique steam engine, India’s first electric locomotive, and various railway-related models, replicas, and the evolution of electrification. Only 12.8 kilometers away from Swapno Puran Resort.
